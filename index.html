How It Works

Four simple steps to recover what's yours.

1
Register
Sign up using your Student ID to access the platform securely.
2
Report
Submit a detailed form with categories, location, and photos.
3
Verification
Security admins review matches and notify the owner.
4
Claim
Collect your item at the SLD office with your ID.
